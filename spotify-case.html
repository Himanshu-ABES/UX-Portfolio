Spotify — Visual discovery and playlist growth

Designed a visually-rich discovery surface that increased exploration and playlist creation via storytelling cards and micro-animations.

Role: Product / UX Designer
Timeline: 2 months

Process

  • Worked with content strategists to define editorial templates and storytelling rules for cards.
  • Designed motion specs to support subtle animated cues and tested perceived delight vs. distraction in user studies.
  • Iterated with data science on recommendation signals that drove playlist saves and exploration.

Design highlights

Story-driven card layouts with progressive reveal of metadata, and motion that communicates affordances (tap to expand, save). Prioritized discoverability while keeping the experience lightweight for mobile.

Artifacts to show

  • Editorial template docs and card spec
  • Motion specs and prototype videos
  • Experimentation dashboards showing saves and playlist growth

Accessibility

Ensured tappable targets met size guidelines, provided accessible labels for interactive card regions and validated behavior for screen reader flows.

Outcome

Anonymized: increase in saves and playlist creation for test groups; improved exploratory behavior and higher satisfaction scores in qualitative testing.
